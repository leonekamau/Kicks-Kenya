Nike Air Max 270 — Product Details

The Nike Air Max 270 delivers an all-day comfort experience with the tallest Air unit in Nike history — a full 32mm of cushioning. The design is inspired by the Air Max 180 and Air Max 93, blending heritage with modern comfort engineering.

  • Upper: Engineered mesh with synthetic overlays
  • Midsole: Air-Sole unit with Max Air heel cushioning (32mm)
  • Outsole: Rubber with flex grooves for natural movement
  • Weight: Approximately 320g (EU 42)
  • Drop: 10mm
  • Fits true to EU sizing
