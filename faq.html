Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping at Kicks Kenya

Authenticity

Yes — without exception. Every pair we sell undergoes a 12-point authentication check before it's listed. We source directly from authorised distributors and use tools like Legit Check App and StockX reference data. If you ever receive a pair that turns out not to be authentic, we'll issue a full refund, no questions asked.

Our lead authenticator, Dennis Mutua, checks each pair against 12 criteria: stitching quality, font consistency, sole shape, insole texture, box label accuracy, lace holes, heel tab, tongue label, serial number, material feel, colour accuracy, and overall weight. We also cross-reference with brand databases and use Legit Check App's AI scanner for a second opinion.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 7-day exchange policy. If you receive the wrong size or your shoes arrive damaged, contact us within 7 days and we'll arrange a free exchange. The shoes must be unworn, in their original box with all tags. We do not accept returns for change of mind once the shoes have been worn.

WhatsApp us at +254 701 234 567 as soon as possible. If the shoes haven't been worn, we'll swap to your correct size free of charge (within 7 days of purchase). If the size you need isn't in stock, we'll issue a store credit valid for 60 days.

Sizing

We list all shoes in EU sizing, which is the international standard used by Nike, Adidas, and most global brands. We also show UK and US equivalents on each product page. If you're unsure, WhatsApp us your foot length in centimetres and we'll recommend the right size.

Generally, Nike runs slightly narrow and true to size in length. Adidas tends to run slightly long — many customers go half a size down in Adidas. New Balance runs wide. We include brand-specific sizing notes on each product page, and our team is happy to advise via WhatsApp.
