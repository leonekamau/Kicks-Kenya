Our Story

From a Westlands market stall to Nairobi's most trusted sneaker destination

Our Roots

Started With One Pair of Jordans

Kicks Kenya was founded in 2019 by Kevin Odhiambo, a sneakerhead who was frustrated by the lack of authentic kicks in Nairobi. The market was flooded with counterfeits, and getting genuine sneakers meant flying to Dubai or paying a premium to grey-market importers.

Kevin started small — reselling authenticated sneakers from his apartment in Westlands, verifying each pair personally before passing them on. Word spread fast in the Nairobi sneaker community. Within a year, demand had outgrown his living room.

Today, Kicks Kenya operates from Sarit Centre in Westlands with a team of 11, stocks over 120 styles, and delivers to all 47 counties. But our core promise hasn't changed: every pair is 100% authentic or your money back.

Sarit Centre, Westlands Road, Nairobi — our home since 2021

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every decision we make

🔬
Authenticity First

Every pair undergoes a 12-point verification check before it hits our shelves. We partner directly with authorised distributors and use Legit Check tools to eliminate all doubt.

🤝
Community Driven

We sponsor Kenya's biggest sneaker meetups and collaborate with local artists for custom colourways. The Nairobi sneaker scene built us — we invest right back into it.

🌍
Proudly Kenyan

We're building the infrastructure for a healthy sneaker market in Kenya — pushing for fair prices, authentic supply chains, and a culture where every Kenyan can access quality footwear.
